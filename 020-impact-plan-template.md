### Step 2 — The rings mapped

Fill in what each ring looks like for your specific goal and role.

| Ring | What it looks like for this goal |

| **Ring 4 — Organization** | [How would you change how the whole company approaches this?] |

### Step 4 — Review rhythm

- **Monthly:** Check this plan against what I am actually doing.
- **Quarterly:** Update the evidence column. Am I making progress?
- **Yearly:** Revisit Step 1 — has my role or goal changed?
